#序理论

>[!definition] 保序映射
>设$(E,\le_E),(F,\le_F)$是[[序关系|有序集]], 一个映射$f:E\to F$若满足对任意$x,y\in E$有
>$$x\le_E y\implies f(x)\le_F f(y)$$
>就称$f$是一个**保序映射**(order homomorphism). 若满足
>$$x\le_E y\implies f(y)\le_F f(x)$$
>就称$f$是一个**反序映射**(anti order homomorphism).

例如, 下图就是一个典型的保序映射.
```tikz
\begin{document}
\begin{tikzpicture}

    \filldraw (0,0) circle (2pt) node[above] {$e$};

    \filldraw (0,-2) circle (2pt) node[above left] {$d$};

    \filldraw (-1,-3) circle (2pt) node[left] {$b$};

    \filldraw (1,-3) circle (2pt) node[right] {$c$};

    \filldraw (0,-4) circle (2pt) node[below] {$a$};

  

    \draw(0,0)--(0,-2);

    \draw(0,-2)--(-1,-3);

    \draw(0,-2)--(1,-3);

    \draw(-1,-3)--(0,-4);

    \draw(1,-3)--(0,-4);

  

    \draw [->,thick] (2,-2) to [bend left=30] node[above] {$f$} (6,-2);

  

    \filldraw (8,0) circle (2pt) node[right] {$f(e)$};

    \filldraw (8,-2) circle (2pt) node[right] {$f(c),f(d)$};

    \filldraw (8,-4) circle (2pt) node[right] {$f(a),f(b)$};\

  

    \draw(8,0)--(8,-2);

    \draw(8,-2)--(8,-4);

\end{tikzpicture}
\end{document}
```

注意, 保序映射可以不是单射, 也可以不是满射, 只要没有出现逆序的点即可.

需要注意的是, 一个保序的双射的逆映射也不一定保序, 故作如下定义:

>[!definition] 保序同构
>$f:E\to F$是保序映射, 若$f$是双射且$f^{-1}:F\to E$也保序, 称$f$为$E$到$F$的**保序同构**(order isomorphism). 称$E,F$是**序同构**的, 记作$E\simeq F$.
