#分析 

>[!theorem] 控制收敛定理 (Dominated Convergence Theorem, DCT)
>设$(X,\mc F,\mu)$是[[测度空间]], 设$(f_n)$是实/复[[可测映射|可测函数]]的[[序列]], 且[[点态收敛拓扑|点态收敛]]到$f$, 即对任意$x\in X$有
>$$f(x)=\lim_{n\to\infty}{f_n(x)}$$
>若存在一个实函数$g\in \ms{L}^1(\mu)$使得对任意$x\in X$有
>$$|f_n(x)|\le g(x),\quad n=1,2,\ldots$$
>则$f\in \ms{L}^1(\mu)$, 并且
>$$\lim_{n\to\infty}{\int_X{f_n\,\d\mu}}=\int_X{f\,\d\mu}$$

>[!proof]
>显然有$|f|\le g$且$f$可测 (可测: [[可测映射|此页面定理5]]), 从而$f\in \ms{L}^1(\mu)$. 因为$|f_n-f|\le 2g$, 对$2g-|f_n-f|$作用[[Fatou引理]], 有
>$$\begin{aligned}\int_X{\liminf_{n\to\infty}{\left(2g-|f_n-f|\right)}\,\d\mu}\le\int_X{2g\,\d\mu}&\le\liminf_{n\to\infty}{\int_X{2g-|f_n-f|\,\d\mu}}\\&=\int_X{2g\,\d\mu+\liminf_{n\to\infty}{\left(-\int_X{|f_n-f|\,\d\mu}\right)}}\\&=\int_X{2g\,\d\mu}-\limsup_{n\to\infty}{\int_X{|f_n-f|\,\d\mu}}\end{aligned}$$
>两边同减$\int_X{2g\,\d\mu}$即有
>$$\limsup_{n\to\infty}{\int_X|f_n-f|\,\d\mu}\le 0$$
>由于$|f_n-f|$非负, 从而
>$$\lim_{n\to\infty}{\int_X{|f_n-f|\,\d\mu}}=0$$
>由[[L1空间|此页面定理1(三角不等式)]], 
>$$\lim_{n\to\infty}{\int_X{|f_n-f|\,\d\mu}}\ge\left|\lim_{n\to\infty}{\int_X{f_n-f\,\d\mu}}\right|=\left|\int_X{f\,\d\mu}-\lim_{n\to\infty}{\int_X{f_n\,\d\mu}}\right|=0$$
>就证明了定理.
