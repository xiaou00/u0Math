#分析 

>[!definition] Jacobi矩阵
>设向量值函数$f:\R^n\supset U\to\R^m$在点$\vec x_0=(x_1,\ldots,x_n)$处可微, 则其[[Fréchet微分]]算子可以表示为:
>$$\vec J_f(\vec x_0)=\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{\p f_1}{\p x_1}(\vec x_0)&\frac{\p f_1}{\p x_2}(\vec x_0)&\cdots&\frac{\p f_1}{\p x_n}(\vec x_0)\\
\frac{\p f_2}{\p x_1}(\vec x_0)&\frac{\p f_2}{\p x_2}(\vec x_0)&\cdots&\frac{\p f_2}{\p x_n}(\vec x_0)\\
\vdots&\vdots&\ddots&\vdots \\
\frac{\p f_m}{\p x_1}(\vec x_0)&\frac{\p f_m}{\p x_2}(\vec x_0)&\cdots&\frac{\p f_m}{\p x_n}(\vec x_0)
\end{bmatrix}$$
>称之为$f$在$\vec x_0$的**Jacobi矩阵**(Jacobian).
