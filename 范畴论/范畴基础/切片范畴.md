#范畴论 
$$
\newcommand{\ob}[1]{\operatorname{ob}{\mathfrak{#1}}}
$$
>[!definition] 切片范畴
>设$\frak C$是一个[[范畴]], $B\in \ob{C}$,  考虑$\frak C$中以$B$为值域的态射, $f:A\to B$, 记作$(A,f)$, 考虑此类元素的全体, 定义态射$h:(A,f)\to(C,g)$当且仅当$\frak C$中态射$h:A\to C$且$f=gh$. 即使下列图表交换:
>```tikz
\usepackage{tikz-cd}
\begin{document}
\LARGE
\begin{tikzcd}[row sep=large] A & C \\ B \arrow["h", from=1-1, to=1-2] \arrow["f"', from=1-1, to=2-1] \arrow["g", from=1-2, to=2-1] \end{tikzcd}
\end{document}
>```
>该范畴称作**切片范畴**(slice categoty over ...), 记作$\mathfrak{C}/B$, 它的[[对偶]]范畴称作**余切片范畴**(coslice categoty over ...), 记作$B/\mathfrak{C}$. 余切片范畴的构造很简单, 就是$\frak C$中以$B$为定义域的态射$f$并记作$(f,A)$, 考虑全体此类元素并定义态射$h:(f,A)\to(g,C)$当且仅当$\frak C$中有态射$h$且$hf=g$. 即使下列图表交换:
>```tikz
\usepackage{tikz-cd}
\begin{document}
\LARGE
\begin{tikzcd}[row sep=large] A & C \\ B \arrow["h", from=1-1, to=1-2] \arrow["f", from=2-1, to=1-1] \arrow["g"', from=2-1, to=1-2] \end{tikzcd}
\end{document}
>```
