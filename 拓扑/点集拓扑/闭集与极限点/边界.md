#点集拓扑 

>[!definition] 边界
>[[拓扑空间]]$X$的子集$A$的**边界**(boundary)定义为:
>$$\bound{A}:=\clos{A}\cap(\clos{X-A})$$
>边界也经常记作$\partial{A}$.

>[!theorem] 边界的性质
>1. $\inte{A}$与$\partial{A}$无交且$\clos{A}=\inte{A}\sqcup\partial{A}$ (即$\partial{A}=\clos{A}-\inte{A}$)
>2. $\partial{A}=\O\iff A$既开又闭
>3. $U$是开集$\iff\partial{U}=\clos{U}-U$.

>[!proof]
> $$\begin{aligned}
> \inte{A}\sqcup\partial{A}&=\inte{A}\sqcup(\clos{A}\cap(\clos{X-A}))\\
> &=(\inte{A}\cup\clos{A})\cap(\inte{A}\sqcup(\clos{X-A}))\\
> &=\clos{A}\cap X=\clos{A}
> \end{aligned}$$
> 
> ---
> $\bound{A}=\O\iff\clos{A}-\inte{A}=\O$, 因此$\clos{A}\subset \inte{A}$, 又由于$\inte{A}\subset A\subset\clos{A}$得$\inte{A}=A=\clos{A}$, 从而$A$既开又闭.
> 
> ---
> $U$是开集$\iff U=\inte{U}\iff\partial{U}=\clos{U}-U$.
> 

>[!warning] 注
>开集的闭包的内部不一定等于其本身. 例如$(1,2)\cup(2,3)$, 其闭包为$[1,3]$, 闭包的内部为$(1,3)$.
