#代数拓扑 

>[!definition] 覆叠空间
>设$p:E\to B$是[[连续映射|连续]]的满射, $U$是$B$的开集. 若$U$的原象$p^{-1}(U)$能表示为$E$中一族互不相交开集$\set{V_\alpha}_{\alpha\in J}$的无交并, 即
>$$p^{-1}(U)=\bigsqcup_{\alpha\in J}{V_\alpha}$$
>并且对每个$\alpha$, $p$在$V_\alpha$上的限制$p|_{V_\alpha}$都是$V_\alpha$到$U$的[[同胚]], 则称$p$**均匀覆盖**$U$. 族$\set{V_\alpha}_{\alpha\in J}$称为$p^{-1}(U)$的**片状分拆**(partition into slice). 如下图所示.
>```tikz
\begin{document}
\begin{tikzpicture}
\filldraw[thick,smooth,fill=white] plot coordinates{(0,0)(-1,1)(0,1.5)(1,1)(1.2,0.8)(0.5,-0.1)(0,0)};
\draw[thick,->] (0.1,2)--(0.1,0.6);
\foreach \r in {1.3,1.6,...,2.7}{
\filldraw[thick,smooth,fill=white] plot coordinates{(0,{\r+0})(-1,{\r+1})(0,{\r+1.5})(1,{\r+1})(1.2,{\r+0.8})(0.5,{\r-0.1})(0,{\r+0})};
}
\node[right] at (0.1,0.8) {$p$};
\node[right] at (1.5,0.8) {$U$};
\node[right] at (1.5,2.5) {$p^{-1}(U)$};
\end{tikzpicture}
\end{document}
>```
>若$B$的每一个点$b$都存在一个邻域$U\ni b$使得$p$均匀覆盖$U$, 则称$p$为一个**覆叠映射**(covering map), 称$E$为$B$的**覆叠空间**(covering space).
