#集合 

>[!theorem] De Morgan定律
>[[交集|交]]的[[补集|补]]等于补的[[并集|并]], 并的补等于补的交. 即$$\begin{aligned}
\complement(A\cap B)=(\complement A)\cup(\complement B)\\
\complement(A\cup B)=(\complement A)\cap(\complement B)
\end{aligned}$$
>这也可以推广到无限并和无限交
>$$\begin{aligned}
\complement{\left(\bigcap_{i\in I}{A_i}\right)}=\bigcup_{i\in I}{\complement{A_i}}\\
\complement{\left(\bigcup_{i\in I}{A_i}\right)}=\bigcap_{i\in I}{\complement{A_i}}
\end{aligned}$$
